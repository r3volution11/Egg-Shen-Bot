# Acknowledgements

This bot wouldn't be what it is today without the help and feedback from some incredible people. I'm grateful for everyone who took the time to test features, provide feedback, and help shape Egg Shen Bot into something genuinely useful for the community.

---

## Special Thanks

### Server Monk & Grizabella

Thank you for the opportunity to help and for being open to providing new experiences on the server. Your willingness to try new things and let me experiment has been invaluable.

### Gorfder (Bork)

Thank you for helping me iron out the bot's initial features and for continuing to give feedback on how to make it useful without being intrusive. Your perspective on server dynamics has kept the bot balanced and practical.

### mildhonestbonsai

You helped create what may be the bot's most unique and cool feature with the tournament bracket system. Your input shaped an entire feature from concept to implementation, and I'm excited to see how communities use it!

---

## To Everyone

Thank you for giving me the opportunity to bounce ideas off you and get your feedback. You're all awesome!

If you've used the bot, reported bugs, requested features, or just said "hey this is cool," you've contributed to making it better. Every bit of feedback helps!
